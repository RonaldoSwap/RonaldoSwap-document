# SALT Basics

**Emission rate:**

- **Reward per block** - 4 SALT
- **Daily emission \(Based on 30k blocks per day\)** - 1,200,00 SALT per day

**Distribution:**

- **Farmers -** 75% of the rewards per block \(4 SALT\)
- **Farmers daily -** 90,000 SALT \(based on 30k blocks per day\)
